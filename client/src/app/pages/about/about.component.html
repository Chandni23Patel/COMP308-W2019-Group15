• Our web application is user friendly and attractive. This web application will be beneficial to patient for booking appointments, medicine prescription and health report.
  • The website will represent all the functionalities that can be performed from Nurse’s and patient’s end.
  • With this application, Nurse can add or delete a patient’s account according to his requirements.
  • Nurse can generate the appointments for the registered patients.
  • Nurse can also reschedule or cancel the appointments for the registered patients on request.
  • Nurse can prescribe medicines to patient’s account so that patient does not have to worry about that.
  • Patient can log in to the account by unique registration number.
